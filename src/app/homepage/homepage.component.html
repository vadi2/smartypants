This is a super basic app to understand the different OAuth workflows.

The following workflows are currently supported:

Workflow Works? How to launch
Provider EHR Launch (practitioner opens the app from within an EHR) ✅ Click link and then hit Launch
Patient Portal Launch (patient opens the app from within patient portal) ✘  
Provider Standalone Launch (practitioner opens the app directly and connects to FHIR) ✘  
Patient Standalone Launch (patient opens the app directly and connects to FHIR) ✘  
Provider Standalone Launch (practitioner opens the app directly and connects to FHIR) ✘  
Backend Service (app connects to FHIR without user login) ✘  
CDS Hooks Service (test your CDS services) ✘ (not a CDS hooks service)
